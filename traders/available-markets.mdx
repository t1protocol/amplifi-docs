## Market source

All markets on Amplifi are sourced from [Polymarket](https://polymarket.com). When you trade on Amplifi, you are buying and selling real Polymarket conditional tokens on the Polymarket CLOB (Central Limit Order Book).

## Market categories

Markets are organized by category, each with different leverage availability:

| Category | Examples | Leverage |
|---|---|---|
| **Crypto** | BTC price above $X on date, ETH monthly close | Up to 10x |
| **Politics** | Presidential elections, congressional races | 1x only |
| **Sports** | NFL, NBA, MLB, soccer outcomes | 1x only |
| **Geopolitics** | Conflict outcomes, diplomatic events | 1x only |
| **Finance** | Stock market milestones, economic indicators | 1x only |
| **Culture** | Awards shows, entertainment events | 1x only |

<Info>
Leverage above 1x is currently available for **crypto markets only**. Other categories will be enabled as the risk engine is validated across more market types.
</Info>

## How markets are added

Markets are curated by the Amplifi team based on:

- **Liquidity:** Sufficient orderbook depth on Polymarket to support leveraged trading
- **Duration:** Longer-duration markets (weeks to months) are preferred — they have deeper orderbooks and more sustained liquidity
- **Volume:** Markets with healthy 24h trading volume

Short-duration or event-driven markets with thin orderbooks (e.g., flash political events, single-game sports bets) may be available at 1x but are not suitable for leverage.

## Crypto markets in detail

The primary leverage-enabled markets are **multi-strike crypto price markets** on Polymarket. These are structured as:

> "Will BTC be above $105,000 on May 23?"

Each event contains multiple strike prices (e.g., $100K, $102K, $105K, $108K, $110K), creating a ladder of binary options around the current spot price. New events are created daily or weekly.

These markets typically have:
- Deep orderbooks ($100K+ in resting liquidity at multiple price levels)
- High 24h volume
- Clear resolution criteria (spot price at a specific time)

## Market resolution

When a Polymarket market resolves:

- **Winning shares** are redeemed at **$1.00** per share
- **Losing shares** are redeemed at **$0.00** per share

Redemption is automatic — you do not need to manually close your position. Amplifi's market resolution service detects the outcome and redeems your shares on-chain via the Conditional Token Framework (CTF).

After redemption:
1. Any outstanding loan is repaid from the proceeds
2. Remaining equity stays in your deposit wallet
3. The position is marked as closed
