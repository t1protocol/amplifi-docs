## Isolated margin

Every position on Amplifi has its own margin. There is no cross-margin or account-level health factor. If one position is liquidated, it does not affect your other positions.

**Your margin = your deposit for that specific position.**

At 5x leverage with $10 margin:
- Position size: $50
- Loan: $40
- Your equity: $10

## Margin and equity

Your **equity** in a position changes as the market moves:

```
equity = (currentPrice × shares) - loanPrincipal - accruedInterest
```

- If the price goes up, your equity increases
- If the price goes down, your equity decreases
- Interest accrual slowly reduces equity over time

Your P&L is the change in equity from your initial margin.

## Liquidation threshold

Amplifi uses a **Layer 1 threshold** of 30%. This means liquidation triggers when your projected post-liquidation equity (after selling, repaying the loan, and accounting for fees) drops to approximately 30% of your original margin.

This is intentionally conservative — you do not lose everything on liquidation. The threshold exists to:

1. Protect the lending pool from bad debt
2. Leave you with some equity after liquidation
3. Provide a buffer for execution slippage and fees

### What affects the liquidation price

| Factor | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Leverage** | Higher leverage = liquidation price closer to entry |
| **Accrued interest** | Tightens the liquidation threshold over time |
| **Orderbook depth** | Thin liquidity pushes the liquidation price closer to the current price |
| **CLOB fees** | Accounted for in the liquidation price calculation (quadratic formula) |

<Warning>
Your liquidation price is **dynamic**. It can move closer to the current price if orderbook liquidity decreases or as interest accrues. Always monitor your positions.
</Warning>

## Example

| | Value |
|---|---|
| Entry price | $0.50 |
| Leverage | 5x |
| Margin | $10 |
| Position size | $50 |
| Shares | 100 |
| Loan principal | $40 |
| Approximate liquidation price | ~$0.43 |

If the price drops from $0.50 to $0.43:
- Shares worth: $43
- Loan: $40
- Remaining equity: ~$3 (30% of original $10 margin)
- Liquidation triggers → shares sold → loan repaid → ~$3 returned to your wallet (minus fees and penalty)

## Adding margin

Currently, you cannot add margin to an existing position. To increase your exposure to a market, open a new position. Existing positions with the same direction may be merged automatically.

## Minimum deposit

The minimum deposit amount is **$1 USDC**.
