## Trading fees

<Info>
**Amplifi currently charges zero trading fees.** There is no platform fee on opening, closing, or managing positions.
</Info>

## Polymarket CLOB fees

When trading on Polymarket's orderbook, standard Polymarket taker fees apply. These are set by Polymarket (not Amplifi) and vary by market.

The taker fee formula is:

```
fee = shares × (feeRateBps / 10,000) × price × (1 - price)
```

Key points:
- Fees are highest when the share price is near \$0.50 (maximum `p × (1-p)`)
- Fees approach zero as the price nears \$0 or \$1
- Some markets (e.g., political markets) may have fees disabled entirely

Amplifi accounts for these fees in all position calculations, including liquidation price computation. Your liquidation price already factors in the cost of selling your shares.

## Lending interest

Leveraged positions borrow from a lending pool. Interest accrues on the loan for the duration the position is open.

- Interest rate is set per pool and may vary by market category
- Interest is denominated in USDC and deducted from your equity at position close
- Accrued interest affects your liquidation price — as interest accumulates, the liquidation threshold tightens

## Withdrawal fee

A flat **\$0.20 USDC** fee is deducted on each withdrawal to cover the Polygon gas cost of the on-chain transaction.

## Liquidation penalty

If your position is liquidated, a penalty is applied to the remaining equity:

```
penalty = (proceeds - loanPrincipal) × penaltyPercent / 100
```

The penalty is transferred to the protocol fee address. The remainder of your equity stays in your deposit wallet.

## Fee summary

| Fee type | Amount | When it applies |
|---|---|---|
| Amplifi trading fee | **\$0** | — |
| Polymarket taker fee | Variable (set by Polymarket) | On every CLOB trade |
| Lending interest | Variable (per pool) | While leveraged position is open |
| Withdrawal fee | \$0.20 USDC | On each withdrawal |
| Liquidation penalty | % of remaining equity | Only on liquidation |
