## Prerequisites

- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet)
- USDC on Polygon (you can bridge from Ethereum or other chains)

## Step-by-step walkthrough

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Go to [app.amplifi.finance](https://app.amplifi.finance) and click **Connect Wallet**. Amplifi supports any EVM wallet via WalletConnect. You will sign a message to authenticate — no on-chain transaction required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit USDC">
    Click **Deposit** and enter the amount of USDC you want to use as trading margin. Your USDC is transferred to your Amplifi deposit wallet on Polygon — a smart contract wallet created specifically for you.

    <Info>
      Minimum deposit is \$2. Your deposit wallet address is deterministic and unique to your account.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse markets">
    Explore available Polymarket markets on the main screen. Markets are organized by category: crypto, politics, sports, and more. Each market shows the current price, 24h volume, and available leverage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a direction and leverage">
    Select a market and choose your position:
    - **Yes** or **No** — your directional bet
    - **Leverage** — from 1x to 10x (crypto markets only; varies by price and liquidity)
    - **Margin** — the amount of your own capital to put up

    The UI shows you the total position size (margin x leverage) and the number of shares you will receive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the preview">
    Before confirming, review the order preview:

    | Field | What it means |
    |---|---|
    | **Entry price** | The price you're buying shares at |
    | **Liquidation price** | The price at which your position will be automatically closed |
    | **Position size** | Total value of your position (margin x leverage) |
    | **Shares** | Number of Polymarket conditional tokens you'll hold |
    | **Fees** | Currently zero — Amplifi does not charge trading fees |

    <Warning>
      The liquidation price is not static. It adjusts based on orderbook depth and accrued interest. If liquidity thins out, your liquidation price moves closer to the current price.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and trade">
    Click **Confirm** to open your position. Amplifi will:
    1. Borrow from the lending pool (if leveraged)
    2. Execute the trade on Polymarket's orderbook
    3. Begin monitoring your position in real-time

    Your position appears in the **Positions** tab immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect after opening

### Monitoring your position
Your position card shows real-time P&L, current market price, and distance to liquidation. The liquidation price is displayed as a red line on the price chart.

### Closing your position
Click **Close** on any open position. Amplifi sells your shares on the Polymarket CLOB at the best available bid, repays any outstanding loan, and returns the remaining equity to your deposit wallet.

### If you get liquidated
If the market price reaches your liquidation threshold:
1. Amplifi automatically sells your shares
2. The loan is repaid from the proceeds
3. A liquidation penalty is applied (percentage of remaining equity)
4. Any leftover equity stays in your deposit wallet

You do not lose your entire margin — the liquidation triggers with equity remaining (the Layer 1 threshold ensures ~30% of your equity is preserved at trigger time, before execution slippage and fees).

### Withdrawing
Click **Withdraw** to move USDC from your deposit wallet back to your connected wallet. Withdrawals are processed on Polygon and typically complete within seconds. A small gas fee (\$0.20) is deducted to cover the Polygon transaction.

## Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Start small" icon="seedling">
    Open a small position first to understand how leverage, liquidation prices, and the close flow work.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Watch the liquidation price" icon="eye">
    It is dynamic. Thin orderbooks push it closer to the current price. Deep liquidity gives you more room.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Use take-profit orders" icon="bullseye">
    Set a take-profit to automatically close your position at a target price without watching the screen.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Understand market resolution" icon="flag-checkered">
    When a market resolves, winning positions pay \$1/share and losing positions pay \$0. You do not need to close manually — redemption is automatic.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
