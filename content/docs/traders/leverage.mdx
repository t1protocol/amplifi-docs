## Overview

Leverage on Amplifi lets you control a larger position than your deposited margin. At 5x leverage, a \$10 deposit controls a \$50 position. The additional \$40 is borrowed from a lending pool and must be repaid when the position closes.

You are trading **real Polymarket shares** — not a synthetic or perpetual contract. Your deposit wallet holds actual conditional tokens on Polymarket's CLOB.

## How it works

1. You deposit \$10 as margin
2. You select 5x leverage on a market priced at \$0.50
3. Amplifi borrows \$40 from the lending pool
4. Your deposit wallet buys 100 shares at \$0.50 (\$50 total)
5. If the price rises to \$0.60, your shares are worth \$60 — a \$10 profit on \$10 margin (100% return vs. 20% at 1x)
6. If the price drops to your liquidation price, Amplifi sells your shares, repays the \$40 loan, and returns remaining equity

## Leverage caps

Maximum leverage depends on several factors:

### Price-based tiers

Leverage is capped by the entry price of the shares. Lower-priced shares have lower max leverage because small price moves represent larger percentage swings.

| Entry price | Max leverage |
|---|---|
| \$0.40+ | 10x |
| \$0.36 – \$0.39 | 9x |
| \$0.32 – \$0.35 | 8x |
| \$0.28 – \$0.31 | 7x |
| \$0.24 – \$0.27 | 6x |
| \$0.20 – \$0.23 | 5x |
| \$0.15 – \$0.19 | 4x |
| \$0.10 – \$0.14 | 3x |
| \$0.05 – \$0.09 | 2x |
| Below \$0.05 | 1x (no leverage) |

### Category restrictions

Currently, only **crypto** markets support leverage above 1x. This includes BTC, ETH, and other cryptocurrency price markets on Polymarket.

Other categories (politics, sports, geopolitics, culture) are available at 1x only. This will expand as the risk engine is validated across more market types.

### Additional caps

- **Spread-based:** If the bid-ask spread is wide, max leverage is reduced. Formula: `floor(1 / (4 × spread))`, clamped between 1x and 10x.
- **Depth-based:** The orderbook must have sufficient bid-side depth relative to your position size. Thin books reduce available leverage.
- **Low-volume markets:** Markets with under \$50k in 24h volume are capped at 2x.
- **Live sports:** Individual live games are capped at 3x regardless of price.

## P&L math

**Long position (buying Yes shares):**

```
Profit = (exitPrice - entryPrice) × shares - fees
ROI    = Profit / margin
```

At 5x leverage, a 10% price increase yields a 50% return on your margin (before fees and interest).

**Important:** Losses are also amplified. A 10% price decrease at 5x leverage means a 50% loss on your margin.

## Liquidation

Every leveraged position has a liquidation price. If the market price reaches this threshold, Amplifi automatically closes the position to protect the lending pool.

The liquidation price is **not static** — it adjusts based on:
- Accrued interest on the loan
- Current orderbook depth
- CLOB taker fees
